A developer and designer. But here's some other stuff.

When I'm not developing sites, I'm teaching yoga classes, running with the West Philly Runners, riding my Jamis Beatnik day or night, blazing heat or freezing cold, reading on my kindle or hanging out with my two amazing cats. I'm as much a designer as I am a fine artist, I love to look at things and arrange them in interesting and beautiful ways. This video has made an impression on my work in a big way: Always be knolling.

Front End Developer

HTML5, CSS3, Javascript, and JQuery are all in my wheelhouse. I have experience coding from designers' PSDs, creating all of the interactive features they can think of using jQuery's functions to creative smooth interfaces.

Project Manager
